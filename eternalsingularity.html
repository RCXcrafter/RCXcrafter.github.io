Eternal Singularity

This is a collaboration with WanionCane & KingWealthyTurtle.

This Minecraft mod is an addon to Singularity mods, such as AOBD Singularities, Thermal Singularities, and Universal Singularities, and it adds an eternal singularity, which acts like a binder, to prevent making the infinity catalyst from Avaritia impossible, due to the lack of crafting space.
