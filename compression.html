Additional Compression

This is a collaboration with KingWealthyTurtle.

This Minecraft mod adds compressed blocks (E.g. Double Compressed Clay, etc., all the way up to Decuple Compression) and is fully compatible with other mods such as Ex Compressum and Extra Utilities.

You can even add more compressed blocks or remove/modify existing ones with the config.
