Paileology

Paileology, the study of buckets.

This Minecraft mod simply adds buckets made out of various materials such as: wood, leather, glass and gold. Wooden buckets can only pick up cold liquids such as water and glass buckets will break after placing a hot liquid such as lava.

The buckets added by this mod are able to pick up liquids from practically any mod so you might never even need iron buckets anymore.

It's also fully configurable which means that you can add and remove buckets to your heart's content.
