The Lemon Taco

The Lemon Taco is a modded Minecraft server that I made and I'm now releasing it publicly for testing. The server is still in alpha, this means: most of the worlds will often be resetted, you are welcome to help me with stuff, a lot of stuff will be changed and stuff may seem a bit unprofessional.

The discord server also functions as a support server for any of my other projects and for contacting me in general.
